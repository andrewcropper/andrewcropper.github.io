Andrew Cropper

I am an associate professor at the University of Helsinki and a PI at ELLIS Institute Finland. My group combines logical reasoning and machine learning. We focus on inductive logic programming (ILP), which learns rules from data. We develop the ILP system Popper. I previously led a research group at the University of Oxford and earned my PhD at Imperial College London.
