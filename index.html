Andrew Cropper

I am an associate professor at the University of Helsinki and a principal investigator at ELLIS Institute Finland.

My group works on combining logical reasoning and machine learning. We focus on inductive logic programming (ILP), a form of symbolic learning that learns human-readable rules. We develop the ILP system Popper.

Previously, I led a research group at the University of Oxford for eight years and earned my PhD at Imperial College London.

I am looking for PhD students, postdocs, master's thesis students, and research interns. If interested, email me at andrew.cropper@helsinki.fi.
