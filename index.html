Andrew Cropper

News About Publications

I am a junior research fellow at Hertford College at the University of Oxford. I design machine learning algorithms that learn to write computer programs. I focus on Inductive Logic Programming (ILP), which combines machine learning and mathematical logic.

I am always looking for collaborators so please email me if you want to work on ILP.
